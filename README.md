# Welcome to Airport Builder!

Airport Builder is **a simple playground where you can use elements to build various airports** you can think of... maybe even real ones? Like Chicago O'hare? Oh, wait, that's not too easy. You can also use this sandboxes to build other types of airports, such as air bases.

Imagine, you were told to build a model airport for a school project, or you wanted to make a Minecraft airport just for fun. That's where **everyone starts from Scratch**. And the best part? **It's completely free!**

## Choosing Your Route

Every airport has a design and purpose. Before you continue, ask yourself:
1. **What type of airport am I going to build?** *(Existing / Commercial Airport / Cargo Hub / Air Base)*
2. **How big will my airport be?** *(Small / Regional / International / Mega Hub)*
3. **What will be the capacity?** *(Depends on how many aprons you put)*

## Placing the First Element

The first element would typically be a runway.
**Step 1:** Click **+ Element → + Runway** to add your first runway.

You should see something like this:

## The Taxiway Network

- A taxiway is used by aircraft to taxi around the airport.
- A ramp is used by aircraft to get in and out of a runway.

**Step 3:** Go to **Element Properties → Taxiway** to place your first taxiway.
